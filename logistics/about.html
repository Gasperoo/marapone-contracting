Who's behind this

WE ARE
MARAPONE.
THIS IS THE DEAL.

Marapone is a B2B AI company based in Canada and Italy, operating globally. We are not a construction company. We are not a venture-backed AI startup burning investor cash on a SaaS dashboard. We are a focused, technical team that builds private AI systems and sells them directly to the businesses that need them.

We operate across two industries because document intelligence has the same fundamental problem in both: critical information buried in hundreds of pages that no one has time to read properly. In construction, it's blueprints, RFIs, and site logs. In logistics, it's trade documents, freight invoices, and compliance filings. The problem is the same. The solution — a private, fine-tuned AI that runs on your own infrastructure — is the same.

Our Canada base gives us deep roots in North American construction and trade. Our Italy presence connects us to European logistics networks and Mediterranean trade corridors. We work primarily in person — on-site with your team — which is how we actually understand the workflow before building anything around it.

Marapone was founded by people with over 10 years of hands-on experience inside construction and logistics — not consultants hired to study the industries from the outside, but people who have worked in them directly. That background matters. It means we already understand the real problems companies in these industries face: the document overload, the coordination failures, the costly assumptions made because no one had time to read page 247 of a blueprint. We didn't need to guess at the pain points. We've lived them.

We don't take on many projects at once. When we're building your system, you have our full attention — not a shared project manager and a ticketing queue. Us, directly, focused entirely on your build and making sure it works perfectly.

We got tired of watching serious businesses get locked into expensive subscription AI that stores their sensitive documents on someone else's servers, then charges them more every month for the privilege. That model benefits the vendor, not the client. We built Marapone to flip that — you pay once, you own everything, we walk away. That's the whole business model.

Domain knowledge

BUILT FROM INSIDE THE INDUSTRY

The people behind Marapone came from hands-on work inside construction and logistics — not from consulting engagements or market research. That changes what we build and how we build it.

CONSTRUCTION

Blueprint ambiguity doesn't surface until it's already cost you

An unclear structural detail, a spec conflict that reads fine on paper, an RFI that never got a definitive answer — these only become visible problems when trades are already mobilizing. The cost isn't just in the rework. It's in the contested change orders and schedule impact that follows. That's the failure mode our tools are built to catch before it happens.

LOGISTICS & TRADE

A single wrong field in a trade document can hold freight for days

Customs errors, invoice discrepancies, and misclassified shipments aren't edge cases — they're a routine cost of operating at volume. We've worked the trade lanes where these failures happen. The document intelligence systems we build are designed around the specific document types and error patterns that actually occur in freight and import/export operations.

ON AI

Generic AI doesn't know your industry — and that gap has real consequences

A model trained on general internet data doesn't understand the difference between a notice to owner and a notice of commencement, or why that distinction matters in a dispute. Industry-specific systems, trained on industry-specific documents, perform differently. That's the premise we build from — and why we fine-tune on your actual project documents, not a generic dataset.

ON OWNERSHIP

You shouldn't need us after delivery — and that's by design

Most software vendors optimize for dependency. We optimize for handover. Every engagement ends with you holding the source code, the model weights, and the documentation. The goal is for the tool to run without us — because that's what real ownership looks like, and it's what we'd want if we were the client.

How we operate

THE WAY WE WORK

01

RADICAL TRANSPARENCY

We tell you exactly what we're building, how it works, and what it can't do. If something won't work for your use case, we'll tell you before you pay — not after. We'd rather lose the deal than waste your time.

02

FULL HANDOVER, ALWAYS

Every engagement ends with you owning everything. Source code. Model weights. Documentation. We keep nothing back. The goal is for you to never need us again after delivery — that's how we know we did the job right.

03

We don't take on many projects at once. Every build gets our full attention and is completed to the highest standard before we move on. Quality isn't negotiable — every client deserves a product that works perfectly.

Important limits to know

WHAT WE CAN'T DO
(AND WON'T PRETEND TO)

We are not a construction company and we are not a logistics operator. We don't know your specific trade, your specific lane, or your local building codes better than you do. What we know is how to build AI systems that read your documents accurately and surface information you'd otherwise miss or find too slowly.

We can't guarantee the AI catches every single issue in every document — no system can, and anyone who claims otherwise is selling you something. What we can guarantee is that it dramatically reduces what slips through, and it does it consistently, without getting tired or distracted.

We are a small, focused team — not a 24/7 support operation. We work during normal hours across our time zones. If you need around-the-clock live support with SLAs, we are not the right fit. We'd rather be honest about that upfront than write you a contract we can't honour.

What we are: a technically rigorous, honest, international team who will give your build genuine attention and deliver something that actually works in your real workflow. That's the whole pitch.

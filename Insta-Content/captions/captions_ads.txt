AD CAMPAIGN NOTES
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PIXEL SETUP (required before running):
Install Meta Pixel on marapone.com. Set up conversion events for:
- Contact form view (/contact page view)
- Form submission (lead event)
- Pricing page view (/pricing page view)
Without pixel data, retargeting (AD 03) and conversion (AD 04) audiences will be limited.

CREATIVE GUIDANCE:
- Use existing brand imagery from campaign (dark backgrounds, orange accents)
- Blueprint/document imagery performs well for construction audience
- Clean number-forward graphics ($18,400, $0.00) perform well for logistics audience
- Avoid stock photos of smiling people in hard hats — too generic for this audience
- Video ads (even 15–30 second clips) typically outperform static for awareness objectives

CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE RECOMMENDATION:
Campaign 1: Awareness (AD 01 + AD 02 in separate ad sets by audience)
Campaign 2: Retargeting (AD 03)
Campaign 3: Conversion (AD 04)

Separate campaigns allow independent budget control and cleaner performance data.

REPORTING METRICS TO TRACK (weekly):
- Cost per link click (Awareness)
- Cost per landing page view (Awareness)
- Cost per form submission/lead (Retargeting + Conversion)
- Frequency (keep below 3 for Awareness; up to 5–7 for Retargeting is acceptable)

// Comparison

MARAPONE
vs AUTODESK CONSTRUCTION CLOUD AI.

Autodesk Construction Cloud (ACC) bundles BIM, document management, and AI features for design-led teams. Marapone is a custom-built private system you own. Different shape, different price, often complementary.

// TL;DR

THREE BULLETS

When ACC AI is best

Your firm is design-build or BIM-heavy, you live inside Revit + ACC, and the AI features tied to model coordination matter most.

When Marapone is best

You're a GC whose pain is in RFI/email/document chaos rather than model coordination, and you want a system that runs on your infrastructure.

The honest middle

ACC for the BIM side, Marapone for the project-administration side. We integrate with ACC as a source.

When ACC AI is the better fit

Stick with ACC if…

  • • Design coordination, clash detection, and BIM-driven workflows are where you spend most of your time.
  • • Your firm is already paying for ACC and the marginal AI cost is small.
  • • You don't need workflows beyond what Autodesk ships.
  • • You're fine with SaaS-only deployment.

When Marapone is the better fit

Pick Marapone if…

  • • Your real pain is RFI backlogs, daily logs, and change-order chaos — not model coordination.
  • • You'd rather run the system on your own hardware with full source code.
  • • You want to ingest from Revit/Bluebeam/Outlook/SharePoint at the same time, in one system.
  • • You're done paying per-user for the same workflows year after year.

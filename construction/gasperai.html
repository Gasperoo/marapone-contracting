MEET GASPERAI

GasperAI is our own AI engine — trained across a range of leading large language models and built from the ground up for the documents that run a jobsite: blueprints, RFIs, change orders, tenders, daily logs. It's the intelligence inside every system Marapone builds for construction, and it runs privately on your infrastructure — not ours.

// What it is

NOT A CHATBOT.
AN INDUSTRY ENGINE.

Generic AI tools are trained to do a little of everything. GasperAI is trained to do one thing extremely well: read, understand, and reason over the documents that move a construction project forward — drawings, specs, RFIs, change orders, tenders, and field reports. It's the core model we fine-tune, wire into a private system, and hand to you.

PURPOSE-TRAINED

Built and tuned on real construction documents, so it understands the language, formats, and edge cases of the trade — not the open web.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

Deployed on the infrastructure we build for you. Your drawings and contracts never train someone else's model and never leave your environment.

YOURS TO KEEP

One-time build, full source code, no subscription. GasperAI ships as part of a system you own outright — desktop and, on Starter and up, in the field.

// What it can do

SIX THINGS GASPERAI
DOES ALL DAY

// Read

DOCUMENT INTELLIGENCE

Ingests drawings, specs, PDFs, spreadsheets and scans, then pulls out the parts that matter — clauses, quantities, dates, parties, line items.

// Cross-check

CONFLICT & GAP DETECTION

Compares drawings against specs and documents against each other to surface mismatches, missing scope, and contradictions before they become disputes.

// Flag

RISK ANALYSIS

Scores documents for risk and ranks what needs a human's eyes first — high-exposure clauses, costly omissions, compliance red flags.

// Extract

STRUCTURED CAPTURE

Turns messy paperwork into clean, structured data your systems can use — registers, tables, and exports instead of re-keying by hand.

// Answer

ASK YOUR DOCUMENTS

Plain-language questions across an entire project file, with answers cited back to the exact drawing, page, and line they came from.

// Draft

DRAFT & SUMMARIZE

Writes the first version of RFIs, responses, summaries and daily logs from the source documents — so your team edits instead of starting from a blank page.

Invoice Verification Engine
Every freight invoice is checked against your contracted rates — line by line. Base freight, accessorial charges, fuel surcharge calculations, dimensional weight, minimum charge provisions. Discrepancies are flagged with the specific contract clause violated and a dispute-ready summary. Your team reviews exceptions, not every invoice. Shipment Document Audit
Before a shipment reaches customs, the system reviews your bill of lading, commercial invoice, packing list, and certificates of origin for internal consistency. Quantity mismatches between documents, description conflicts, valuation discrepancies, missing certifications — all flagged before filing. One undetected discrepancy can trigger a customs hold that delays an entire shipment. The system catches them before that happens.

Customs Entry Review
Import entries are screened for HTS classification accuracy, customs valuation methodology, country-of-origin declarations, and applicable trade agreements. Entries with elevated compliance risk are flagged for human review before submission. Particularly valuable for businesses managing multiple origins, first sale valuation, or complex product classifications.

Inbound Risk Scoring
Each incoming shipment receives a risk score based on documentation completeness, carrier performance history on the lane, compliance exposure indicators, and value concentration. Your operations team gets a prioritized queue — high-risk shipments get immediate attention, clean shipments clear efficiently.

The system is trained on your carrier agreements, your trade lanes, your historical shipment data. Private deployment. Full ownership. No ongoing fees.

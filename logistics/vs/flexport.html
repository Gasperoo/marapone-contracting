// Comparison

MARAPONE
vs FLEXPORT WORKFLOWS.

Flexport bundles freight forwarding services with a workflow + visibility platform. They book your freight; the software comes with the relationship. Marapone is private AI for forwarders and brokers who want to keep their own carrier relationships and add intelligence on top.

// TL;DR

THREE BULLETS

When Flexport is best

You're a shipper looking for a forwarder, you want their service to handle execution, and the platform is a free benefit on top.

When Marapone is best

You're a forwarder, broker, or shipper who books your own freight, and you want AI to automate audit, scoring, and rate work without changing carriers.

The honest middle

If you book through Flexport, the platform is included. If you book through your own panel, you need a different stack — Marapone is built for that case.

When Flexport is the better fit

Pick Flexport if…

  • • You're a shipper who'd rather hand off forwarding to a third party.
  • • You don't have your own carrier panel and don't want to build one.
  • • The platform is a benefit you don't need to pay for separately.
  • • Their lane coverage matches your needs.

When Marapone is the better fit

Pick Marapone if…

  • • You're a forwarder or broker — Flexport is a competitor, not a vendor.
  • • You have or want to keep your own carrier relationships.
  • • You want AI workflows that work across whatever forwarders, carriers, and TMS you choose.
  • • You want to own the system rather than depend on a service relationship.

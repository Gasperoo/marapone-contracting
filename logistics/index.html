SIX AI SYSTEMS.
ONE APPROACH.

Every system is built around your specific documents — your trade lanes, your commodity codes, your carrier network. Not a generic platform. Not a dashboard you rent. A private AI that belongs to you.

Freight Invoice Intelligence

INVOICE AUDITOR

Automatically cross-references carrier invoices against rate confirmations, accessorial charges, and contracted rates. Flags billing discrepancies before you pay them — not after.

  • ✓Accessorial charge validation
  • ✓Rate vs. contract comparison
  • ✓Duplicate shipment detection

Import/Export Document Processing

TRADE DOC ENGINE

Reads bills of lading, commercial invoices, packing lists, and certificates of origin — extracts structured data, validates cross-document consistency, and flags discrepancies before they reach customs.

  • ✓Multi-document cross-validation
  • ✓HS code consistency check
  • ✓Structured data extraction

Customs Risk Scanner

COMPLIANCE GUARD

Scans customs entry packages for compliance risks — misclassified goods, undervaluation signals, origin document gaps, and permit/license requirements — before filing, not after a hold.

  • ✓Tariff classification review
  • ✓Valuation consistency check
  • ✓Permit & license flag

Rate Comparison Engine

FREIGHT RATE AI

Parses carrier rate quotes and spot rates from any format — PDF, email, spreadsheet — normalizes them against your contracted lanes, and identifies overcharges and negotiation opportunities automatically.

  • ✓Multi-carrier rate normalization
  • ✓Lane-by-lane comparison
  • ✓Historical benchmark analysis

Carrier Performance Analytics

CARRIER TRACKER

Aggregates delivery confirmation data, POD documents, and exception reports across your carrier base. Generates automated scorecards, on-time performance trends, and claim rate analytics for carrier reviews.

  • ✓Automated carrier scorecards
  • ✓Claim rate tracking
  • ✓Exception pattern analysis

Supplier Communication Intelligence

SUPPLIER INTEL

Processes supplier confirmations, shipment notices, and order acknowledgements — extracts key dates, quantities, and commitments, flags deviations from POs, and builds a structured shipment timeline automatically.

  • ✓PO deviation detection
  • ✓Shipment timeline extraction
  • ✓Multi-language document support

01
Construction Ontario, Canada Pilot Build

BLUEPRINT AUDITOR
FOR A RESIDENTIAL GC

The problem

A residential general contractor managing 12–18 active projects at a time was losing 6–8 hours per week manually cross-referencing blueprint revisions against active scopes of work. Revision conflicts were catching them after trade mobilization — at cost. They needed something that could flag scope drift and dimension conflicts before the crews showed up.

What was built

A private Blueprint Auditor system, installed on a dedicated machine at their office. The system ingests PDF blueprints, extracts dimension data, room specs, and scope indicators, then cross-references revisions to flag conflicts and changes. Output is a structured audit report — specific page, specific conflict, specific delta — formatted for their project coordinator to act on directly.

How it runs today

Their project coordinator drops blueprints into a watched folder and the report lands in their shared drive within minutes. No login, no cloud upload, no monthly invoice from us. The system has been running independently for seven months.

FREIGHT INVOICE
INTELLIGENCE SYSTEM

The problem

A domestic freight broker processing 300–400 carrier invoices per month was manually validating each one against rate confirmations. Their ops team was spending nearly five hours a week just on validation — and still missing overcharges. A billing error from a carrier had gone undetected for three months before they caught it in a quarterly audit.

What was built

A Freight Invoice Intelligence system that reads incoming carrier invoices (PDF and EDI exports), extracts charge line items, and cross-references them against rate confirmations stored in their local drive. Discrepancies are flagged in a structured exceptions report with exact variance amounts. The system runs on a dedicated machine in their office — carrier invoices never leave the premises.

How it runs today

Invoices land in a watched folder, the exceptions report is ready within two minutes. Their ops team reviews flags instead of reading every line. Running for five months with one retainer call to add a new carrier template.

03
Trade & Logistics Northern Italy Professional Build

CUSTOMS COMPLIANCE
DOCUMENT VALIDATOR

The problem

A mid-size Italian trading company managing cross-border shipments into and out of the EU was dealing with compliance errors on customs packages — wrong HS codes, missing certificate references, mismatches between commercial invoices and packing lists. Errors weren't caught until broker submission, causing delays and re-filing costs. Their compliance team was reviewing packages manually, and documents were sometimes in three different languages.

What was built

A multilingual Customs Document Validator, handling Italian, English, and German source documents. The system reads complete customs packages — commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, CMR — and validates them for internal consistency, required field presence, and HS code plausibility. Errors are returned as a structured pre-submission checklist before the package goes to their broker.

How it runs today

Their compliance officer runs packages through the system before broker submission. Re-filing incidents dropped significantly in the first quarter. The system is deployed on their internal server and handles the full document volume without external API calls or cloud processing.

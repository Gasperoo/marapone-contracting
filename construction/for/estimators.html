WHERE THE HOURS GO

PAIN 01

Hand takeoffs eat 60% of the bid

Three days clicking through PDFs to count fixtures, measure runs, tally door schedules. By the time you have quantities, you've spent the budget for actually thinking about the bid.

→ FIX: Live Takeoff & Cost Intelligence reads the drawings, runs the takeoff, and pulls live unit costs from your historical jobs and supplier data.

PAIN 02

Five sub quotes, none comparable

Mech sub A excludes controls, mech sub B includes them but assumes a different sequence, mech sub C didn't read the addendum. You spend an afternoon trying to make them apples-to-apples and you still miss something.

→ FIX: Subcontractor Scope Gap Finder normalizes every quote against the spec, marks every inclusion and exclusion, and shows you the real comparison.

PAIN 03

Spec page 380 ate your margin

An onerous warranty clause, a custom liquidated damages provision, an unusual schedule sequence — buried where nobody read. You won the job. Then you found it. Then the contingency was gone before mobilization.

→ FIX: Tender Package Risk Scanner reads the entire spec and flags every clause that deviates from your standard baseline, ranked by exposure.

PAIN 04

Addenda land 48 hours before close

Addendum 4 changes three sections, deletes one, adds a new spec for the lobby. You have to re-do the takeoff, re-issue to subs, re-level. You barely make submission with a number you don't fully trust.

→ FIX: The build re-runs the affected takeoff regions and re-levels the impacted scopes automatically, so addenda are minutes not hours.

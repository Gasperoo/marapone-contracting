// What we build for logistics & trade

LOGISTICS AI
THAT YOU OWN

Seven purpose-built AI modules for freight, import/export, and trade compliance operations. Each built around your specific documents, your trade lanes, and your workflow — not a generic SaaS platform you're renting by the month.

Freight Invoice Intelligence

INVOICE AUDITOR

Carrier invoices are riddled with accessorial charges, rate discrepancies, and duplicate billing that most teams catch too late — if at all. The Invoice Auditor processes every freight invoice against your rate confirmations, contracted rates, and historical benchmarks. It flags overcharges, duplicate shipments, and unapproved accessorials automatically, before payment runs.

Built around your specific carrier contracts and lane agreements — not generic freight industry rates. The AI knows your negotiated rates and enforces them on every invoice without manual review.

Get a free invoice audit demo →
Import/Export Document Processing

TRADE DOC ENGINE

Every international shipment generates a stack of documents that need to be consistent with each other: the commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading, certificate of origin, and customs entry all reference the same goods but are typically created separately by different parties. Discrepancies between them cause customs delays, holds, and penalties. The Trade Doc Engine processes the full document set, extracts structured data, and validates cross-document consistency before the package reaches customs.

Handles PDF, scanned documents, Excel, and structured data exports. Processes multi-page trade packages in under 30 seconds.

See a document processing demo →
Customs Compliance

COMPLIANCE GUARD

Customs holds and penalties cost significantly more than the time it takes to check compliance before filing. The Compliance Guard scans your customs entry packages against your specific commodity types, import/export destinations, and applicable trade agreements — flagging tariff classification inconsistencies, valuation issues, origin documentation gaps, and permit requirements before you file.

Configured for your specific trade lanes — Canada, US, EU, or custom. The rules engine is updated as your operations change, not locked to a generic compliance database.

Request a compliance scan demo →
Rate Intelligence

FREIGHT RATE AI

Carrier rate quotes come in every format imaginable — emailed PDFs, Excel attachments, portal screenshots, and structured EDI feeds. Comparing them manually against your contracted rates and historical lane data is time-consuming and error-prone. The Freight Rate AI ingests quotes in any format, normalizes them against your contracted lanes, and surfaces the actual cost comparison your team needs to make the right call — including all-in cost with accessorials, not just the base rate.

Book a rate analysis demo →
Carrier Intelligence

CARRIER TRACKER

Most operations track carrier performance manually — pulling PODs, logging exceptions, building spreadsheets for quarterly reviews. The Carrier Tracker processes delivery confirmations, exception reports, and claims documents automatically, building continuous performance scorecards for every carrier in your network. Know which carriers are trending before their performance becomes a problem — not after a shipper complaint.

See carrier analytics demo →
Supplier Intelligence

SUPPLIER INTEL

Supplier communications — order confirmations, shipment notices, packing notifications, and booking confirmations — arrive in every language and format and need to be cross-referenced against your purchase orders. The Supplier Intel module processes the full supplier communication chain, extracts committed quantities and ship dates, flags PO deviations, and builds a running shipment timeline automatically — without your team manually translating and logging each document.

Multi-language support included — if your suppliers communicate in Mandarin, Italian, Spanish, or German, the AI handles extraction without manual translation.

Use Case 07 Advanced

LIVE SUPPLY CHAIN TWIN

A real-time digital replica of your supply chain, built on live API feeds from carrier networks, port authorities, and global data sources. Every active shipment is tracked continuously. When anything changes — a port congestion event, a weather disruption, a carrier failure — the system detects it within seconds and immediately calculates alternative routing options. You're notified before the delay becomes a problem, not after.

By the time most teams learn about a disruption, the window to reroute has already closed. This closes that gap completely — running privately on your own infrastructure, with no third-party exposure of your shipment data.

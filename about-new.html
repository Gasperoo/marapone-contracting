// Who's behind this

WE ARE MARAPONE.
THIS IS THE DEAL.

We are a specialized team based in Canada and Italy, operating globally. We are not a construction company. We are not a venture-backed AI startup. We are a boutique team that builds AI tools and sells them directly to the people who need them.

We got tired of watching good construction teams get pitched on subscription AI that costs $800/month, stores their blueprints in someone else's cloud, and vanishes when the VC funding dries up. That's not a tool — that's a rental with bad landlord terms.

So we build differently. We take your docs, we fine-tune a model specifically on them, we build a local app that runs on your machine, and then we hand you everything — source code, model weights, install guide — and walk away. You own it. You control it. We're out of the loop.

We take one client at a time. That means when we're building your tool, you have our full attention. Not a project manager's Slack message. Ours.

We work primarily with mid-size construction GCs doing $500k–$5M projects — the folks who are big enough to have real document pain but not big enough to have an in-house tech team to deal with it. Much of our work is done remotely, so location is no barrier. If that's you, we should talk.

// How we operate

THE WAY WE WORK

RADICAL TRANSPARENCY

We tell you exactly what we're building, how it works, and what it can't do. If something won't work for your use case, we'll tell you that before you pay — not after.

FULL HANDOVER, ALWAYS

Every engagement ends with you owning everything. Code. Model weights. Docs. We keep nothing. You should never need us again after the build is done — that's the point.

ONE CLIENT AT A TIME

We don't take on three builds simultaneously and ship mediocre work to all three. One build, full attention, done right. Then the next one.

NO FAKE STATS

No "12+ agents deployed." No "over $5B in projects managed." No generic team photos. This is us, our tools, and our clients. That's it.

// Important limits to know

WHAT WE CAN'T DO
(AND WON'T PRETEND TO)

We are not a construction company. We don't know your specific trade or your local building codes better than you do. What we know is how to build AI systems that read documents accurately and surface information you'd otherwise miss.

We can't guarantee the AI catches every single issue in a blueprint — no tool can. What it does is dramatically reduce the number of things that slip through, and it does it faster than any human review.

We are also not available around the clock. We are a small, focused team. We work during normal hours. If you need 24/7 support, we are not the right fit — and we'd rather tell you that upfront than pretend we can offer that.

What we are: a technically solid, honest, international team based in Canada and Italy who will give your project real attention and hand you something that actually works. That's the whole pitch.

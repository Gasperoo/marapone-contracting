COOKIE POLICY

Most cookie policies are long because companies use a lot of cookies. Ours is short because we don't. Here's exactly what's on this site and why.

Effective date: January 1, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 2026  ·  Marapone Contracting Inc. (Canada)

The short version: this website uses only the cookies it needs to function. No advertising cookies. No tracking pixels. No third-party surveillance. No behavioural profiling. We do not know who you are from your visit alone, and we prefer it that way.

01 — What Cookies Are

A QUICK EXPLANATION

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They are widely used to make websites work, remember your preferences, and — in many cases — track behaviour across the internet for advertising purposes.

We use cookies only for the first reason: making the website work correctly. The other uses don't fit our model or our values.

02 — Cookies on This Site

WHAT'S ACTUALLY ON THIS SITE

ESSENTIAL / FUNCTIONAL COOKIES

These cookies are required for basic site functionality. Without them, parts of the site may not work as expected. They do not track you across other websites and contain no personally identifiable information.

Cookie Purpose Duration Type
_session Maintains your session state while navigating the site (e.g., tab selections on pricing page) Session (deleted when browser closes) First-party
cf_clearance Set by Cloudflare (our hosting/CDN provider) for security and bot protection. Does not track personal behaviour. 30 days Third-party (Cloudflare)
__cf_bm Cloudflare bot management cookie. Distinguishes humans from automated bots to protect site integrity. 30 minutes Third-party (Cloudflare)

COOKIES WE DO NOT USE

✗ Advertising / targeting cookies — We run no paid advertising campaigns that require tracking your behaviour. No Facebook Pixel, Google Ads conversion tags, or similar.
✗ Analytics cookies — We do not use Google Analytics, Hotjar, Mixpanel, or any other behavioural analytics platform that tracks individual user journeys on this site.
✗ Social media tracking cookies — No social share buttons with embedded trackers. No LinkedIn Insight Tag. No Twitter/X pixel.
✗ Cross-site tracking — We do not participate in any network that tracks your browsing across other websites.

03 — Third-Party Services

EXTERNAL SERVICES THIS SITE USES

This site loads a small number of external resources. Each is limited to functional delivery of the site and none are used for tracking or advertising:

Google Fonts

We load typography (Bebas Neue, DM Sans, DM Mono) from Google Fonts. This results in a request to Google's servers when you load the page. Google may log this request. No cookie is set by Google Fonts. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Tailwind CSS CDN

The site loads Tailwind CSS from a CDN. This is a stylesheet delivery request only. No personal data or cookies are involved.

Cloudflare

We use Cloudflare for site delivery and security. Cloudflare may set the cookies described in Section 2 above. Cloudflare's privacy policy applies to data processed by their network: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

04 — Your Control

HOW TO MANAGE OR DISABLE COOKIES

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • —View all cookies currently stored on your device
  • —Delete individual cookies or all cookies from a specific site
  • —Block cookies from specific websites or third parties
  • —Block all cookies (note: this may affect functionality on many websites)

05 — Legal Basis

GDPR & LEGAL BASIS FOR COOKIES

For visitors from the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, our legal basis for the essential cookies described in this policy is legitimate interest — specifically, the interest in operating a functional, secure website. These cookies do not process personal data in a way that overrides your rights and freedoms.

We do not use cookies that require your consent under GDPR (i.e., non-essential advertising or tracking cookies). If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated and an appropriate consent mechanism will be implemented before any such cookies are placed.

06 — Changes

UPDATES TO THIS POLICY

If we ever add new cookies to this site — for any reason — we will update this policy before doing so. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. We will not introduce non-essential cookies without updating this policy and, where required by law, obtaining your consent first.

// Comparison

MARAPONE
vs CHATGPT ENTERPRISE.

ChatGPT Enterprise is a remarkable general-purpose model. It writes great emails. It does not, on its own, automate your RFI workflow or stay aware of your specific projects. Here's the honest framing.

// TL;DR

THREE BULLETS

When ChatGPT Enterprise is best

You want a powerful general assistant for your office staff to draft emails, summarize meetings, write proposals, brainstorm — across many topics, not just construction.

When Marapone is best

You want automation that ingests your specific project documents, runs end-to-end without copy-paste, and never sends those documents to a third party.

The honest middle

Many of our clients keep ChatGPT Enterprise for general writing and use Marapone for the workflows that touch sensitive project data. Different jobs, both useful.

When ChatGPT Enterprise is the better fit

Stick with ChatGPT if…

  • • Your AI use is mostly general — writing, brainstorming, summarizing.
  • • You're comfortable with cloud-based processing and OpenAI's enterprise terms.
  • • You don't need workflows that touch your full project data continuously.
  • • You want one tool for many tasks rather than a custom build.

When Marapone is the better fit

Pick Marapone if…

  • • You want automated workflows, not a chat box.
  • • Sensitive drawings, owner emails, or fee schedules cannot leave your network.
  • • You want the AI to know your projects without re-explaining them every conversation.
  • • You're done paying per-seat and want to own the system.

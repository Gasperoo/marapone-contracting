// The engine inside your logistics build

MEET GASPERAI

GasperAI is our own AI engine — trained across a range of leading large language models and built from the ground up for the documents that move freight: bills of lading, packing lists, invoices, customs paperwork, carrier records. It's the intelligence inside every system Marapone builds for logistics, and it runs privately on your infrastructure — not ours.

// What it is

NOT A CHATBOT.
AN INDUSTRY ENGINE.

Generic AI tools are trained to do a little of everything. GasperAI is trained to do one thing extremely well: read, understand, and reason over the documents that move a shipment forward — bills of lading, packing lists, commercial invoices, freight bills, and customs paperwork. It's the core model we fine-tune, wire into a private system, and hand to you.

PURPOSE-TRAINED

Built and tuned on real logistics documents, so it understands the language, formats, and edge cases of freight — not the open web.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

Deployed on the infrastructure we build for you. Your shipment data never trains someone else's model and never leaves your environment.

YOURS TO KEEP

One-time build, full source code, no subscription. GasperAI ships as part of a system you own outright — desktop and, on Starter and up, in the field.

// What it can do

SIX THINGS GASPERAI
DOES ALL DAY

// Read

DOCUMENT INTELLIGENCE

Ingests BOLs, invoices, PDFs, spreadsheets, scans and emails, then pulls out the parts that matter — references, quantities, dates, parties, line items.

// Cross-check

CONFLICT & GAP DETECTION

Compares documents against each other — BOL against packing list against invoice — to surface mismatches and missing detail before they ship.

// Flag

RISK & AUDIT

Scores documents for risk and catches overcharges, duplicate charges, and compliance red flags that need a human's eyes first.

// Extract

STRUCTURED CAPTURE

Turns messy paperwork into clean, structured data your TMS can use — rates, terms, and references instead of re-keying by hand.

// Answer

ASK YOUR DOCUMENTS

Plain-language questions across an entire shipment file, with answers cited back to the exact document, page, and line they came from.

// Draft

DRAFT & SUMMARIZE

Writes the first version of responses, summaries and exception notes from the source documents — so your team edits instead of starting from a blank page.

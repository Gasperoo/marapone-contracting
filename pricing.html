// Common questions

THINGS PEOPLE ASK

Does the AI send my blueprints or trade docs anywhere?

No. The model runs locally on your machine or your server. Your documents never leave your network. That's literally the point.

Do I need a GPU or expensive hardware?

No. It runs on a regular Windows or Mac laptop with no special hardware. For bigger doc volumes, a mid-range workstation helps, but it's not required for the Starter or Pilot tiers.

What if I need it for a new project later?

The tool works on any docs you feed it — it's not locked to one project. If you want to re-fine-tune on a new project's specific doc set, we'll quote a small additional build fee.

How long does the build take?

Typically 2–4 weeks from when you send us your doc sets. We'll give you a specific estimate on the discovery call.

Is the ongoing support plan required?

Not at all. The software is yours — you can run it indefinitely without ever paying us again. The support plans exist for companies that want someone on call, have active projects running year-round, or have passed their 90-day included support window and want continued access.

Can I see it running before I pay?

Yes — that's exactly what the free 24h assessment is for. Send us one real PDF. We'll run it through the AI, and you'll see actual output before spending anything.

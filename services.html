For Construction Companies

CONSTRUCTION AI SERVICES

These are tools built for general contractors, developers, and project managers. Each one targets a specific problem that currently eats hours of your team's time every week. No technical setup required on your end — we build it, deploy it, you use it.

📐

Use Case 01

Blueprint Review Before You Bid

You receive a 200-page PDF blueprint before a bid is due. Instead of your team spending days reading it, the AI reads the entire thing in minutes and gives you a plain-language summary: what's in scope, what's missing, what's contradictory, and what questions you need answered before you commit to a number.

The problem it solves: catching scope gaps before they become a $40,000 change order you can't recover.

📋

Use Case 02

RFI Backlog — Cleared in Hours

Got 50, 70, 100+ open RFIs piling up? The AI reads every single one, checks them against your blueprints, and tells you which ones are blocking trades right now, which are aging too long, and which answers actually changed something critical. Your team stops firefighting and starts managing.

The problem it solves: nobody on your team has time to manage the RFI log properly — so things slip until they're emergencies.

📓

Use Case 03

Daily Logs → One Weekly Report

Your superintendent files a daily log every single day. That's 20+ logs a month, per project. Nobody reads all of them — until there's a dispute and suddenly you need to prove exactly when a delay started and who caused it. The AI reads all of them and sends a weekly summary: manpower trends, weather impacts, recurring delays, and anything that could matter in a claim.

The problem it solves: losing disputes because the evidence was in the logs — but nobody was reading them.

🔍

Use Case 04

Subcontractor Scope Gap Finder

You've got scope letters from five different subs. The AI compares each one against the full spec and drawings to find items that everyone assumes someone else is doing — but nobody actually priced. These gaps are what cause finger-pointing and cost overruns after mobilization.

The problem it solves: "I thought that was in your scope" conversations that cost real money.

📝

Use Case 05

Tender Package Risk Scanner

Before you submit a bid, run the entire tender package through the AI. It flags ambiguous specifications, one-sided contract clauses, items that need clarification from the owner, and anything that's unusual compared to standard project documents. You go into every tender with eyes open.

The problem it solves: signing contracts with traps in them that only become visible six months into construction.

⚠️

Use Case 06

Change Order Risk Tracker

Feed in your change order log and the AI reviews each one for cost exposure, missing scope detail, unapproved items, and changes that could create downstream conflicts with other trades. You know which COs need more documentation before they go to billing — and which ones are clean.

The problem it solves: change orders that get disputed at close-out because the documentation wasn't there.

🗓️

Use Case 07

Meeting Minutes Action Tracker

Upload your site meeting minutes, progress reports, or owner's meeting notes. The AI extracts every action item, who owns it, and what was promised — and flags anything that was discussed verbally but not documented properly. No more "that's not what was said in the meeting" surprises.

The problem it solves: verbal commitments that disappear because nobody wrote them down properly.

🏁

Use Case 08

Deficiency List Management

Got multiple inspection reports with overlapping deficiency lists? The AI consolidates them, removes duplicates, tracks what's been closed, what's still open, and what keeps appearing repeatedly — pointing at a deeper problem. Close-out moves faster when you know exactly where you stand.

The problem it solves: close-out delays caused by nobody having a complete, current picture of outstanding deficiencies.

For Logistics & Import/Export Companies

LOGISTICS & TRADE AI SERVICES

These tools are built for importers, exporters, freight brokers, customs brokers, and supply chain teams. International trade generates enormous document volume. Every document is a potential error, a compliance risk, or a billing mistake. We make the AI read them so your team doesn't have to.

🗂️

Use Case 01

Shipment Document Audit

Before filing customs, run your full shipment package through the AI — bill of lading, commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin. It checks that all the numbers, weights, quantities, and descriptions match across every document. Mismatches get flagged before they cause a customs hold or a penalty.

The problem it solves: a $200 data entry mistake on an invoice turning into a 2-week customs delay on a $400,000 shipment.

💸

Use Case 02

Freight Invoice Verification

Upload your carrier invoices alongside your rate agreements. The AI compares what was charged against what was contracted — line by line. It catches overcharges on freight rates, fuel surcharges above your agreed cap, duplicate billing, and extra fees that appear on shipments that shouldn't carry them.

The problem it solves: paying for freight errors you'd only find if someone had time to check every invoice — which nobody does.

🛃

Use Case 03

Customs Entry Review

Before submitting a customs entry, the AI checks your commodity classifications, declared values, country of origin, and required certificates against your actual shipping documents. It flags what's missing, what's inconsistent, and what might trigger a review — before the filing, not after.

The problem it solves: customs exams and penalties that stem from preventable documentation errors.

📡

Use Case 04

Supplier Communication Digest

Every week, dozens of supplier and carrier emails come in — booking confirmations, status updates, exception notices, rate changes. The AI reads them all and produces a single weekly digest: what shipments are on track, what's delayed, what needs a decision from you, and what's been promised but not confirmed.

The problem it solves: critical supplier updates getting buried in email threads until it's too late to act.

📊

Use Case 05

Trade Lane Performance Report

Feed in your historical invoices and shipment records across your trade lanes. The AI summarizes which lanes are performing well, which carriers are consistently slow or overbilling, and where you're paying above-market rates. Gives you the data you need to renegotiate contracts or switch carriers with evidence behind you.

The problem it solves: renewing carrier contracts without actually knowing how the carrier performed.

⚖️

Use Case 06

Import/Export Compliance Check

Trade regulations change. Tariff classifications get updated. Licensing requirements shift. The AI reviews your trade documents against the applicable rules for your specific commodities and trade corridors, and flags potential compliance gaps before they become fines, license suspensions, or shipment seizures.

The problem it solves: regulatory changes that your team didn't catch because nobody had time to track them.

🚨

Use Case 07

Inbound Shipment Risk Scoring

Every week, get a scored risk summary of your active inbound shipments — which ones have documentation problems, which carriers have a pattern of delays on this lane, and which shipments are most likely to be short, held, or late. Your team stops reacting to problems and starts preventing them.

The problem it solves: supply chain disruptions that were visible in the data — if anyone had time to look at it.

📣

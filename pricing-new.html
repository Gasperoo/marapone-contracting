// Common questions

THINGS PEOPLE ASK

Does the AI send my blueprints anywhere?

No. The model runs locally on your machine or your server. Your docs never leave your network. That's literally the point of this whole thing.

What if I need it for a new project after the build?

The tool works on any docs you feed it — it's not locked to a single project. If you want to re-fine-tune on a new project's specific doc set, we can talk about a small additional build fee.

Do I need a GPU or expensive hardware?

No. It's designed to run on a regular Windows or Mac laptop with no special hardware. For bigger doc volumes, a mid-range workstation helps, but it's not required for the Pilot tier.

How long does the build take?

Typically 2–4 weeks from when you send us your doc sets. We'll give you a specific estimate on the discovery call.

Can I see it running before I pay?

Yes — that's exactly what the free 24h audit is for. Send us one of your real PDFs, we'll run it, and you'll see actual output before spending anything.

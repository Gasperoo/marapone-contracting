// The four things eating your margin

WHERE BROKERS LOSE

PAIN 01

Rates live in 40 spreadsheets

Carrier A emailed updated FAK rates Tuesday. Carrier B updated their fuel surcharge model last month and you forgot to refresh the sheet. Every quote is a guess on whether the rate you're using is the rate you'll pay.

→ FIX: Freight Rate AI ingests every carrier rate sheet, surcharge update, and contract addendum, and quotes against the live picture in seconds.

PAIN 02

Invoice audits never happen

200 carrier invoices a week. Detention, accessorials, fuel adjustments, weight reweighs. Manually checking each against the quote is two days of someone's life — so it doesn't happen, and 3-5% of the invoice spend is wrong in the carrier's favour.

→ FIX: Invoice Auditor reads each invoice line against the quoted rate and the contract, surfaces every discrepancy with evidence, and lets you dispute in batches.

PAIN 03

Carrier scorecards are six months stale

A carrier's on-time rate dropped to 62% over the last quarter and nobody noticed because the scorecard hasn't been updated since April. By the time you catch it, your shipper customer already has.

→ FIX: Carrier Tracker pulls performance data from your TMS, EDI feeds, and POD records continuously — the scorecard updates itself.

PAIN 04

RFQ turnaround is losing you bids

A shipper sends a 30-lane RFQ. Your competitors quote in two hours. You quote in two days because pricing each lane is a manual lookup. The fast broker wins the test load, then the relationship, then the contract.

→ FIX: The build prices the full lane list against your live carrier panel and pulls margin recommendations in minutes — you respond same-hour.

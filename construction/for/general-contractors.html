// The four things that eat your week

WHERE THE TIME GOES

PAIN 01

RFIs sit for 8+ days

Your team forwards the same drawing question three times. The architect responds in fragments. By the time you have a clean answer, the trade is back-charging delays.

→ FIX: RFI Intelligence reads the question, the relevant drawing region, and prior responses, then drafts a structured answer pack you forward in one click.

PAIN 02

Daily logs are 3 lines, all weather

Supers hate logs because they're 20 minutes of typing for no obvious benefit. So they write nothing — until you need a defensible record for a claim and there isn't one.

→ FIX: Daily Log Synthesizer turns a 90-second voice memo into a structured log with crew counts, deliveries, blockers, and photo captions.

PAIN 03

Change orders turn into disputes

Three months in, you're chasing the email that proves the owner agreed to the scope shift. The PM left. The PE thinks it was verbal. The trade is invoicing anyway.

→ FIX: Change Order Risk Tracker continuously scans your email, RFIs, and meeting minutes for scope drift and pre-builds the paper trail.

PAIN 04

Closeout deficiencies pile up

Substantial completion is a moving target because the deficiency list is in someone's notebook, plus a punch app, plus three emails from the consultant.

→ FIX: Deficiency List AI consolidates every photo, note, and consultant report into one ranked list — and re-checks itself as items are closed.

// What it is

NOT A CHATBOT.
AN INDUSTRY ENGINE.

Generic AI tools are trained to do a little of everything. GasperAI is trained to do one thing extremely well: read, understand, and reason over the documents that move construction and logistics work forward — blueprints, RFIs, change orders, bills of lading, customs paperwork, invoices, and the rest. It's the core model we fine-tune, wire into a private system, and hand to you.

PURPOSE-TRAINED

Built and tuned on real construction and logistics documents, so it understands the language, formats, and edge cases of your trade — not the open web.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

Deployed on the infrastructure we build for you. Your documents never train someone else's model and never leave your environment.

YOURS TO KEEP

One-time build, full source code, no subscription. GasperAI ships as part of a system you own outright — desktop and, on Starter and up, in the field.

// What it can do

SIX THINGS GASPERAI
DOES ALL DAY

// Read

DOCUMENT INTELLIGENCE

Ingests PDFs, drawings, spreadsheets, scans and emails, then pulls out the parts that matter — clauses, quantities, dates, parties, line items.

// Cross-check

CONFLICT & GAP DETECTION

Compares documents against each other and against the spec to surface mismatches, missing scope, and contradictions before they become disputes.

// Flag

RISK ANALYSIS

Scores documents for risk and ranks what needs a human's eyes first — high-exposure clauses, costly omissions, compliance red flags.

// Extract

STRUCTURED CAPTURE

Turns messy paperwork into clean, structured data your systems can use — tables, registers, and exports instead of re-keying by hand.

// Answer

ASK YOUR DOCUMENTS

Plain-language questions across an entire project or shipment file, with answers cited back to the exact page and line they came from.

// Draft

DRAFT & SUMMARIZE

Writes the first version of RFIs, responses, summaries and logs from the source documents — so your team edits instead of starting from a blank page.

// Built for two industries

SAME ENGINE.
TWO TRADES.

Construction

WHAT IT DOES ON SITE

  • ▸Blueprint & spec review — reads drawings and specifications, flags conflicts, missing details, and code concerns.
  • ▸RFI & change-order analysis — drafts RFIs, tracks the backlog, and assesses change-order risk and exposure.
  • ▸Tender & scope gap finding — scans tender packages for risky terms and scope that's been left out.
  • ▸Daily logs & deficiencies — turns field notes into structured daily logs and deficiency lists.
  • ▸Meeting minutes & tracking — captures decisions and action items, and keeps them moving.

Logistics

WHAT IT DOES IN THE LANE

  • ▸BOL & packing-list checks — cross-checks bills of lading, packing lists, and commercial invoices before they ship.

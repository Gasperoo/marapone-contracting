HOW THE
SYSTEM WORKS

Every system we build is a private, fine-tuned AI trained on your specific documents — not a generic chatbot you throw files at. It's built to do one job well: save your team hours of document review every week, on your hardware, with zero external dependencies.

Construction Intelligence Suite

C1

BLUEPRINT AUDITOR

YOUR 300-PAGE
BLUEPRINT,
ACTUALLY READ

Drop in a PDF — architectural drawings, structural plans, mechanical/electrical specs, or any combination. The AI reads the entire document, not just the text layer, and returns a structured audit report with real findings.

Scope extraction

Pulls out key scope items: floor areas, structural elements, material specs, phasing notes — listed clearly, not buried in page 247.

Conflict detection

Flags when architectural drawings say one thing and structural specs say another — the kind of thing that causes $40k change orders if missed in bid.

Ambiguity flagging

Identifies vague or underspecified items that need clarification before you mobilize trades. Stops assumptions from becoming delays.

Example output

"Structural drawing S3.2 specifies W250×89 beams for grid line 4. Architectural A2.1 shows a depth of 200mm at the same location — dimensional conflict requiring RFI."

C2

RFI INTELLIGENCE

YOUR RFI LOG,
ACTUALLY MANAGED

Feed it your full RFI backlog — sent, received, answered, unanswered. It reads every one, cross-references against the blueprints, and tells you exactly what's at risk.

Critical path mapping

Which open RFIs are sitting on your critical path? The AI identifies them specifically, based on the trade sequence in your specs.

Response status tracking

Identifies which RFIs have been unanswered longest and flags aging items before they become formal delay claims.

Example output

"RFI #42 (masonry window lintel spec) submitted 18 days ago. No response received. Framing for Level 3 windows cannot proceed without answer. Critical path impact: est. 3–5 days if not resolved by week-end."

C3

DAILY LOG SYNTHESIS

WEEKS OF LOGS,
ONE CLEAR REPORT

Your site super files a daily log every day. That's 20+ logs a month per project. Nobody reads all of them. The AI does — and surfaces what matters.

Weekly rollup

Turns 5 days of daily logs into one clean summary: manpower counts, weather delays, deficiency notes, and anything that looks like a pattern.

Delay pattern detection

If the same trade is consistently logging short crew sizes on Mondays, or a supplier is mentioned in delay notes three weeks running — it connects those dots.

Claims protection

If it comes to a dispute, having delay events systematically documented and cross-referenced is worth a great deal. This builds that record automatically.

C4

DELAY & CASH-FLOW SIGNALS

WARNINGS BEFORE
THEY'RE EMERGENCIES

Not an accounting system. Not a scheduling tool. Just simple, early signals from your docs — when your draw schedule is at risk and why, before it becomes a conversation you're not prepared for.

Delay cluster analysis

When 3+ delay events cluster in the same phase, it flags the potential draw impact before you're having that conversation with the bank.

Risk summary per phase

Each project phase gets a simple risk score — low / medium / high — based on what's in your RFIs, logs, and blueprint conflicts combined.

Supply chain tie-in

Reads delivery confirmation delays, customs hold notifications, and supplier communications that impact material arrival and site schedule.

Logistics & Trade Intelligence Suite

L1

TRADE DOCUMENT PROCESSOR

YOUR TRADE DOCS,
VALIDATED & CLEAR

Bills of lading, commercial invoices, packing lists, certificates of origin, customs declarations — the AI reads every document in your shipment package, validates fields against each other, and flags errors before they become customs problems.

Cross-document validation

Checks that quantity, weight, value, and description data is consistent across your BL, commercial invoice, and packing list. Catches mismatches before they cause holds.

Missing field detection

Flags required fields that are blank, inconsistent, or using non-standard formats for your specific lane and commodity type.

Shipment summary

Extracts a clean summary of each shipment — origin, destination, commodity, value, carrier, ETD/ETA — without manually reading every page of every doc.

Example output

"Commercial invoice line 3 shows 450 units. Packing list shows 430 units for same line. Bill of lading shows 450 cartons. Discrepancy on packing list — confirm before customs filing."

L2

FREIGHT INVOICE AUDIT

CARRIER INVOICES,
ACTUALLY CHECKED

Most freight invoices go unpaid for weeks and unchecked indefinitely. The AI reads every carrier invoice against your contracted rates, flags billing discrepancies, and surfaces duplicate charges before you pay them.

Rate vs contract audit

Compares charged rates to your agreed tariffs, spot quotes, and contracts. Flags any lane where the invoice differs from what was quoted.

Accessorial charge review

Identifies detention, fuel surcharge, and accessorial line items that exceed contracted caps or appear on shipments that shouldn't carry them.

Duplicate detection

Catches the same shipment being invoiced twice across a carrier's billing cycle — a surprisingly common source of freight spend leakage.

Example output

"Invoice #INV-2847 — fuel surcharge applied at 24.5%. Contracted rate for this lane: 18.2%. Overcharge of $312 on this shipment. Flag for carrier dispute."

L3

COMPLIANCE & SUPPLY CHAIN RISK

RISK SIGNALS,
BEFORE THEY LAND

Customs holds, compliance failures, and supplier delivery problems don't come out of nowhere — they're readable in your documents before they happen. This module reads across your inbound doc set and surfaces what's at risk.

Compliance document monitoring

Reviews import/export documentation for your specific commodity classifications, country of origin rules, and regulatory requirements. Flags gaps before filing.

Supplier communication analysis

Reads supplier emails, booking confirmations, and shipment status messages to surface lead-time risk and delivery threats early — before your operations feel the impact.

Shipment risk scoring

Each inbound shipment gets a risk profile: compliance completeness, delivery confidence, and documentation quality — so your team knows where to focus attention.

Example output

"Shipment #SHP-441 — certificate of origin missing for CA-US preferential tariff claim. Filing without it will result in standard duty rate. Est. additional cost: $4,200. Action required before customs entry."

WHAT THIS IS NOT

✗

Not a full ERP or TMS

It reads your documents and flags issues. It doesn't replace your project management system, your accounting platform, or your transportation management system.

✗

Not a live data connector

It processes documents you feed it — PDFs, spreadsheets, emails. It doesn't pull live data from carrier APIs or port systems in real time.

✗

Not a generic AI chatbot

It doesn't answer vague questions or hallucinate answers. It reads specific documents and returns structured outputs based on what's actually in them.

✗

Not a cloud SaaS subscription

No logins. No monthly fee. No dashboard you rent. It's a local application that runs on your hardware and produces structured reports. Built once, runs indefinitely.
